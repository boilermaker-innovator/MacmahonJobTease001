Full Job Description

Position: Experienced Boilermaker

Location: Tropicana Gold Mine, 330km east-northeast of Kalgoorlie, Western Australia

Company: Macmahon - A leading Australian contract mining company

About the Role:

Join the Tropicana Gold Mine Project team as an EXPERIENCED Boilermaker responsible for repairing and fabricating metal components of various Surface MOBILE Mining equipment. FIFO from Perth with a 2:1 Days/Nights roster and 7% market uplift bonus paid quarterly.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Repair and fabricate any metal components
  • Perform welding, gouging, cutting or other duties as specified by Supervisor
  • Complete weekly bucket and blade condition reports
  • Access metal required to repair or fabricate components
  • Undertake laboring duties as required

Requirements:

  • Previous experience in a similar role, preferably within mine site environment
  • Australian Recognised Trade Certificate
  • Experience with on-site repairs (e.g., excavator bucket and truck tray body rebuilds)
  • Proficiency in welding (MIG/TIG/STICK), air arc gouging, cutting, and lancing
  • Ability to read and work according to engineering drawings and work plans
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills
  • Commitment to Macmahon safety culture and values
  • Australian Manual Driver's License (mandatory)

Benefits:

  • Competitive remuneration & rewards
  • Ongoing career development opportunities
  • Dedicated lifestyle, financial, and mental health support
  • Salary sacrifice options for flights, vehicles & airport parking
  • Employee discounts with Macmahon's retail, vehicle, insurance, and banking partners

Macmahon is an equal opportunity employer committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace.
